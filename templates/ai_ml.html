Predictive Modeling with LSTM

TradeAlchemy leverages Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks, a specialized type of Recurrent Neural Network (RNN) capable of learning long-term dependencies.

Unlike standard neural networks, LSTMs have "memory cells" that can retain information over time, making them ideal for predicting stock prices where past trends heavily influence future movements.
